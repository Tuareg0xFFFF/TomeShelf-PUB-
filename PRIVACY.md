# Privacy Policy for TomeShelf

Last updated: April 4, 2026

TomeShelf is a native Audiobookshelf client for iOS, iPadOS, and macOS. Audiobookshelf is a self-hosted audiobook and podcast server. This policy explains how TomeShelf handles your data.

## Data Collection

TomeShelf does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer or any third party. The app does not include analytics, tracking, or advertising.

## How Your Data Is Used

All data in TomeShelf stays between your device and the Audiobookshelf server you configure. This includes:

- Server connection details (URL, username, and authentication token)
- Library metadata (book titles, authors, cover images)
- Playback progress
- Downloaded audiobook and podcast files

Authentication tokens are stored securely in the iOS Keychain on your device. Downloaded files and cached data are stored locally on your device.

## Network Communication

TomeShelf communicates only with the Audiobookshelf server you configure. No data is sent to any other server. The app does not make network requests to the developer or any third-party service.

## Data Storage and Deletion

All app data is stored locally on your device. You can delete your data at any time by:

- Removing a server from the app (deletes all associated data)
- Disabling offline library (deletes downloaded files)
- Deleting the app (removes all stored data, including Keychain entries)

## Children's Privacy

TomeShelf does not collect data from any user, including children under 13.

Updates to this policy will be posted on this page with a revised date.
